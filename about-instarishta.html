About InstaRishta

InstaRishta is a trusted Muslim matrimony platform designed to help individuals and families find verified nikah proposals through a simple, transparent and mobile-friendly experience.

Why families use it

The site is built around family-first discovery: clear profile metadata, verified badges, renewal dates, and a contact flow that can stay private or guardian-led when needed.

How the data stays fresh

Profiles are created and edited locally in the JSON editor, exported as a clean jsdata.json file, and then published back to the site so content freshness is easy to manage.

Trust signals

  • Visible verified badges on public cards
  • Family approval and wali-led contact for safer introductions
  • Private contact flow with prefilled respectful messages
  • Urgent listings with renewal and expiry support

Why this page matters

This page improves discoverability for Google Search, AI answer engines, and voice assistants by targeting clear intent and entity context for Muslim matrimony and nikah matchmaking.
