The Day He Forgave All His Enemies: A Masterclass in Mercy

Arrasool Team

Updated Oct 2024 • 5 min read

"He who does not show mercy to others, will not be shown mercy by God."

For thirteen years, the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) and his followers were tortured, boycotted, and eventually driven out of their homes in Mecca. Yet, when he returned years later as a conqueror with an army of ten thousand, history expected a bloodbath...

The Moment of Truth

Standing at the Kaaba, he looked at those who had killed his relatives and mocked his message. He asked them: "What do you think I am going to do with you?" They replied, "You are a noble brother."

"Go, for you are all free."

This act alone converted thousands. It wasn't the sword that conquered Mecca; it was the character (Akhlaq) of the Messenger of Allah.
